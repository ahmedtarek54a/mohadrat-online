About Mohadrat Online

Mohadrat Online are designed to make learning accessible, modern, and friendly. This static demo uses LocalStorage for light interactions.

Give learners and instructors a focused, simple tool for delivering and accessing educational content.

Vision

Be a lightweight platform that can be extended to a full product with backend, auth and real video storage.

Core Features

  • Discussion forum with persistent comments
